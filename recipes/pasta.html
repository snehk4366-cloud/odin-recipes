Broccoli Walnut Pasta

Description

This broccoli walnut pasta is a bright, rustic pasta dish. It's a simple but flavor-packed main or side dish that comes together with basic staples but tastes anything but basic.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces orecchiette pasta
  • 3 cups small broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, roughly chopped
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup Sweety Drop peppers, drained
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup feshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook according to package directions. During the last 2 to 3 minutes of cooking, add broccoli. Reserve 1 cup pasta water; drain..
  2. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add walnuts and cook, stirring often, until lightly toasted and fragrant, 3 to 5 minutes. Transfer walnuts to a plate.
  3. In the same skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until lightly golden, about 1 minute. Stir in sweety drop peppers and red pepper flakes; cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add pasta and broccoli to the skillet. Toss to coat, adding reserved pasta water a little at a time until a light sauce forms.
  5. Remove from heat. Add lemon juice, walnuts, and Parmesan; toss until combined and glossy. Taste; season with salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, and additional lemon juice to taste.
